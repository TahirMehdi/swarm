Clear canvas About

1. Click on screen to select points.

2. Whenever 3 points are selected - blue parallelogram and yellow circle will appear.

3. Tooltip with points coordinates and shapes area are in the left bottom corner of the screen.

4. You can drag any selected point to change shapes. Shapes will recalculate accordingly.

5. Use 'Reset' button to clear the board.

6. Use 'About' button to open this dialog again.

7. Click at any point of the screen to close this dialog.
